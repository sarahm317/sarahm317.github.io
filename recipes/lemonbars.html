Lemon Bars

This is my absolute favorite lemon bars recipe. In fact, there's an entire subreddit dedicated to this recipe.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 227 grams (1 cup) butter
  • 65 grams (1/2 cup) powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 6 eggs
  • Juice of 5 lemons
  • Zest of 1 lemon

  • (48 grams) 6 tablespoons flour
