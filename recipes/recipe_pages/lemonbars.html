Lemon Bars

This recipe is my twist on Reddit's favorite lemon bar. I found this recipe one day while browsing on the baking subreddits. Turns out there is an entire subreddit dedicated to just this recipe. I increased the amount of lemon filling to make a thicker bar. I also added some vanilla, lemon zest, and a little salt in the shortbread crust for extra flavor.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags: desserts pastries bars

instructions

step 1

Line the bottom of the pan with parchment paper. Preheat oven to 350°F.

step 2

Cream the butter, 2 cups of flour, powdered sugar, and 1 teaspoon of salt in a stand mixer. Press the mixture into the lined pan. With a fork, poke a few holes in the crust. Bake the crust for 20 minutes or until slightly golden on top.

step 3

While the crust is baking, prepare the lemon filling. In a stand mixer, beat the eggs. Add the zest of 1 lemon and the juice of all the lemons. Beat until mixed. Add the granulated sugar, 6 tablespoons of flour, 3/4 teaspoon of salt, and vanilla. Beat until mixed

step 4

Remove the crust from the oven when done cooking. While the crust is still hot, pour the lemon mixture over the crust. Bake for 30 minutes. Sprinkle the lemon bars with powdered sugar while still hot. Wait until the lemon bars are fully cooled before cutting.

ingredients

3 cups flour

1 1/2 cups unsalted butter (room temperature)

3/4 cup powdered sugar

1 teaspoon salt

6 eggs

2 1/2 cups sugar

6 tablespoons flour

3/4 teaspoon salt

6 lemons

2 teaspoon vanilla extract

supplies

Stand mixer (or hand mixer)

9 x 13 inch glass baking pan

Parchment paper

Zester/Grater
